Pentaduo — Privacy Policy

Last updated June 28, 2026

Pentaduo is private by default. We don't run any servers we control, we don't have user accounts, and we don't collect, store, sell, or share your personal information. There is no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking in the app.

What data the app uses, and where it stays

  • On your device. Your settings, match history, daily-challenge results, puzzle progress, and stats are stored locally on your device.
  • In your private iCloud (optional). If you're signed into iCloud with iCloud enabled for Pentaduo, that same gameplay data syncs across your devices through your own private iCloud database (Apple's CloudKit). It lives in your iCloud account; we have no access to it. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Game Center (optional). If you use leaderboards and achievements, Apple's Game Center shows your Game Center nickname and your scores on global leaderboards and records your achievement progress. Apple handles this under Apple's Privacy Policy; the app only submits your scores and reads public rankings.
  • Daily Challenge winning boards (public). When you win the Daily Challenge while signed into Game Center, your finished board layout, your Game Center nickname, your score, and the difficulty/moves/time of that win are published to a shared public iCloud (CloudKit) database so other players can view the day's top scorers' boards and study their strategy. This is the only data Pentaduo makes publicly visible to other players. Only a winning first attempt is published; losses, practice replays, and your other gameplay data are never shared. To avoid publishing, sign out of Game Center before playing the Daily Challenge.
  • Multiplayer (optional). When you play Nearby (local) or Online multiplayer, your display name and your in-game moves are sent directly to the other player's device(s) to run the match — over the local network (Nearby) or through Game Center (Online). Nothing is routed through a server we control.
  • Reminders (optional). If you turn on daily reminders, the notifications are scheduled on your device. Nothing leaves your device to enable them.

What we never collect

  • Your name, email, contacts, photos, or location.
  • Advertising identifiers or any cross-app tracking data.
  • Analytics or usage telemetry of any kind.

Children's privacy

Pentaduo does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. Game Center and iCloud features are controlled by the device's Apple ID and Screen Time settings.

Your choices

  • Turn off iCloud sync for Pentaduo in Settings → [your name] → iCloud.
  • Disable Game Center in Settings → Game Center (this also stops any Daily Challenge winning board from being published).
  • Turn off reminders in the app's settings.
  • Deleting the app removes its on-device data; anything already synced to your iCloud is managed in your iCloud settings.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
