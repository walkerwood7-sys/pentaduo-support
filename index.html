Pentaduo

Card & board strategy match

Pentaduo is a single-player and multiplayer card-and-board strategy game for iPhone and iPad — five-in-a-row across two decks, eight jacks, and four wild corners. This page is here to answer questions and help you reach me if something goes wrong.

Frequently asked

Does the game work offline?

Yes. The single-player game, daily challenge, stats, and tutorial all work without a network. You only need a connection for Game Center online multiplayer and iCloud sync.

How do I sync between iPhone and iPad?

Sign in to the same iCloud account on both devices. The single-player in-progress match, match history, and your daily challenge streak sync automatically via CloudKit. Game Center stats sync via Game Center.

How does Game Center multiplayer work?

Make sure Game Center is signed in (Settings → Game Center on iOS). Then open Pentaduo → Multiplayer → Game Center, invite a friend or auto-match. Both players replay the same deterministic shuffle, so neither side has an advantage.

How is the AI rated?

Four tiers. Novice plays mostly random legal moves. Apprentice uses simple run-and-block heuristics. Adept looks one ply ahead. Expert runs alpha-beta search with a richer evaluation under a ~1.5-second time budget per move — Expert plays a serious game.

Can I delete my data?

Pentaduo doesn't collect personal data for itself, and there are no accounts. The only thing that's ever shared with other players is your optional Daily Challenge winning board, and only if you win while signed into Game Center (see Privacy). To wipe local stats and match history: iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Pentaduo → Offload App, then reinstall.
